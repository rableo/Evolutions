Lisa Barnes, LCSW

Hello, I am Lisa Barnes and I have over 25 years of experience helping children and adults manage and resolve life issues. Stress can come from a traumatic event, relationships, academic/work, loss, and life transitions such as aging, preganancy and divorce. Whatever you are experiencing, we can work together to use the tools you already have and evidenced based practices to evolve your mental health and enjoy your life.
