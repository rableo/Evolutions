I do not use one particular method of therapy but tailor treatment to the specific needs of the clients. Modalities include EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing), CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy), Attachment, Regulation, & Competency (ARC), Emotion-Focused Couples Therapy (EFT), Narrative Therapy, and Theraplay©. When working with children and adolescents I include parents whenever appropriate, particularly when issues are related to attachment and trauma. By far the most valuable tools I have are God’s Word, prayer, and the guidance of the Holy Spirit.
